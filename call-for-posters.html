Overview

We invite researchers, students, and practitioners to submit AI research posters aligned with our 2026 conference theme: Building Scalable AI That Works: From Research to Deployment in Resource-Constrained Environments.

Poster presentations provide a platform to share original research, applied studies, and deployment experiences, with a strong focus on real-world impact in low-resource settings, particularly in Africa and Nigeria.
