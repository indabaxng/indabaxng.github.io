Call For Papers

IndabaX is a locally organised Indaba (i.e., gathering) that aims to enhance knowledge and build capacity in machine learning and artificial intelligence across individual countries in Africa. The theme for this year's discourse revolves around "AI-Driven Innovations for the Digital Economy in Nigeria." With the rapid evolution of artificial intelligence (AI) technologies, the landscape of the digital economy is undergoing profound transformations. These innovations have the potential to revolutionize various sectors, including finance, healthcare, agriculture, and education, among others. However, amidst these advancements, it's crucial to ensure that AI-driven solutions remain focused on enhancing the human experience and contributing positively to societal progress. As AI becomes increasingly integrated into our daily lives and economic systems, there arises a pertinent question: How can we leverage AI-driven innovations to foster inclusive growth and empower individuals within the digital economy? Unlike traditional approaches where technology may seem distant and opaque, the key lies in placing human values, needs, and experiences at the forefront of AI development and deployment. We welcome submissions from the general public. These could be any of:

  • Paper presentations
  • Workshops and code-labs
  • Poster presentations
It's okay if this is your first time speaking or presenting at a conference, we all have a first time. Topic Ideas

If you don't know what topics to pick, please consider some of the following:

  • AI-Powered Financial Inclusion Initiatives
  • Leveraging AI for SME Growth and Innovation
  • Data-driven Healthcare Solutions in the Digital Age
  • AI Applications in Agricultural Development and Food Security
  • Smart Cities: Enhancing Urban Living with AI Technologies
  • Ethical Considerations in AI Adoption for Economic Development
  • AI-driven Education and Skill Development Programs
  • AI-enabled E-commerce and Consumer Behavior Analysis
  • Blockchain and AI Integration for Transparent Supply Chains
  • AI-driven Energy Management and Sustainability Solutions
  • Enhancing Cybersecurity with AI and Machine Learning
  • AI-driven Tourism and Hospitality Industry Transformations
  • AI Applications for Environmental Conservation and Climate Change Mitigation
  • AI-powered Transportation and Logistics Optimization
  • Regulatory Frameworks for AI Adoption in the Digital Economy
Submission Guidelines

Research papers must be original and on topics at the intersection of AI/ML and health, education, security, privacy, or other related areas.

  • Paper submissions should be in double-column ACM format (excluding the bibliography and well-marked appendices) and not more than 12 pages.
  • Submissions must be in English and properly anonymized (non-identifying information in datasets).
  • All accepted Abstract submissions will be presented at the workshop in a poster session.
  • One author of each accepted paper will be required to attend the conference to present the paper for it to be included in the proceedings. However, they do not have to attend the workshop in person. They can present their paper remotely.
BEST PAPER AWARD

We look forward to your outstanding contributions. There will be an award for the best paper selected by the reviewers.
