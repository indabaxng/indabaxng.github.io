Call for Reviewers & Area Chairs

IndabaX Nigeria 2026

Conference Proceedings & Poster Review

IndabaX Nigeria 2026 invites applications for Reviewers and Meta-Reviewers / Area Chairs to support a rigorous, fair, and high-quality peer-review process for conference papers and poster submissions.

Reviewers

Reviewers will evaluate conference paper submissions and/or poster submissions, depending on their expertise and availability. Reviews will focus on technical soundness, originality, clarity, relevance to the conference theme, and overall contribution, with an emphasis on providing constructive and actionable feedback.

Note: Reviewers may indicate their preference to review conference papers only or posters. Different review workflows or platforms may be used for paper and poster reviews.

Meta-Reviewers / Area Chairs

Meta-Reviewers / Area Chairs oversee the review process for assigned tracks, covering conference papers and/or poster submissions. Responsibilities include moderating reviewer discussions, ensuring review quality and fairness, and providing final recommendations.

Note: Area Chairs may also indicate whether they are available to oversee papers, posters, or both, based on experience and capacity.

Review Process

Selected reviewers and area chairs will be invited to the appropriate conference management and review platforms, where they will participate in the review, discussion, and decision-making processes for conference papers and poster submissions.
